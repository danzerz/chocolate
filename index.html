Toblerone

Toblerone is a Swiss chocolate brand known for its distinctive shape, which is a series of joined triangular prisms. The chocolate is made from Swiss milk, honey, and almond nougat.

About

  • Toblerone was created by Theodor Tobler in 1908.
  • The shape of Toblerone is believed to be inspired by the Matterhorn mountain in Switzerland.
  • Toblerone is available in various flavors, including milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and white chocolate.
  • The name Toblerone is a portmanteau combining Tobler's name with "torrone," the Italian word for honey and almond nougat.
